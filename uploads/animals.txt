Animals need food, water, and shelter.
Some animals live in forests.
Birds can fly.
Fish live in water.

African Elephant can weigh over 6,000 kg and uses its trunk for breathing, smelling, drinking, and grasping objects.
Cheetah can reach speeds of around 100–120 km/h in short bursts.
Blue Whale can grow longer than 30 meters and weigh more than 150 tons.
