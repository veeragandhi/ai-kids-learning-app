Dinosaur Knowledge Sheet Overview Dinosaurs were a diverse group of reptiles that lived during the Mesozoic Era. They  first appeared approximately 230 million years ago during the Late Triassic Period  and dominated terrestrial ecosystems until the end of the Cretaceous Period about 66 million years ago. The Mesozoic Era is divided into three periods: Triassic (252–201 million years ago) Jurassic (201–145 million years ago) Cretaceous (145–66 million years ago) Not all prehistoric reptiles were dinosaurs. Flying pterosaurs and marine reptiles  such as plesiosaurs and mosasaurs belonged to separate groups. Tyrannosaurus rex Tyrannosaurus rex lived during the Late Cretaceous Period approximately 68–66  million years ago. Characteristics: Length: 12–13 meters Weight: 8–10 tons Diet: Carnivore Habitat: Western North America Bite force estimated above 35,000 newtons Interesting fact: Despite its enormous size, T. rex possessed relatively small arms with only two  functional fingers. Triceratops Triceratops lived during the Late Cretaceous Period. Characteristics: Length: 8–9 meters Weight: 6–12 tons Diet: Herbivore Distinctive feature: Three facial horns Large bony frill behind the head Triceratops and T. rex lived during roughly the same geological time period. Velociraptor 
Velociraptor lived approximately 75–71 million years ago. Characteristics: Length: Around 2 meters Weight: Around 15 kilograms Diet: Carnivore Habitat: Present-day Mongolia Contrary to popular movies, Velociraptor was much smaller than portrayed and likely  had feathers. Stegosaurus Stegosaurus lived during the Late Jurassic Period approximately 155–150 million  years ago. Characteristics: Length: 9 meters Weight: 4–5 tons Diet: Herbivore Distinctive feature: Double row of plates along the back Tail spikes called "thagomizers" Stegosaurus lived millions of years before T. rex. Brachiosaurus Brachiosaurus lived during the Late Jurassic Period. Characteristics: Length: 22–26 meters Weight: 30–50 tons Diet: Herbivore Unlike many sauropods, Brachiosaurus had longer front legs than hind legs, giving it a giraffe-like posture. Spinosaurus Spinosaurus lived approximately 99–93 million years ago. Characteristics: Length: 14–18 meters Weight: 7–20 tons Diet: Primarily fish 
Distinctive feature: A large sail-like structure formed by elongated neural spines on its back. Recent evidence suggests Spinosaurus spent significant time in aquatic environments. Ankylosaurus Ankylosaurus lived during the Late Cretaceous Period. Characteristics: Length: 6–8 meters Weight: 6–8 tons Armor: Osteoderms embedded in skin Weapon: Large tail club Ankylosaurus is considered one of the most heavily armored animals ever discovered. Geological Timeline 230 million years ago: First dinosaurs appear. 201 million years ago: Jurassic Period begins. 155 million years ago: Stegosaurus flourishes. 150 million years ago: Brachiosaurus common in North America. 99 million years ago: Spinosaurus present in North Africa. 75 million years ago: Velociraptor lives in Mongolia. 68 million years ago: Tyrannosaurus rex emerges. 
66 million years ago: Mass extinction event eliminates non-avian dinosaurs.
